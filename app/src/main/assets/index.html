Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers regarding our child-friendly formulas, procedures, warranties, and rapid response slots.

Is the treatment safe for children and pets?

Yes, we use government-approved, low-odor, child and pet-safe chemicals. Our modern odorless gels and strategic baits target bugs precisely without releasing airborne residues or toxic vapors.

How long does the treatment last?

Between 30 days and 1 year depending on the specific service and pricing package chosen. Our Standard option provides 90 days of complete warranty coverage, while the Annual Maintenance Contract (AMC) keeps your property shielded all 365 days.

Do you offer emergency same-day service?

Yes, 24x7 emergency booking is available via call or WhatsApp. If you notice a sudden termite outbreak, wasp swarm, or heavy infestation, we can deploy a trained technician to your home or shop right away.
