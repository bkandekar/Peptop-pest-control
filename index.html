Frequently Asked Questions

Clear, direct answers regarding our elite formulas, procedures, warranties, and emergency protocols.

Are the protective agents safe for domestic pets and kids?

Absolutely. All Peptop solutions are green, plant-derived, or fully organic bio-reagents. They are specifically engineered to eliminate target insect metabolisms without causing harm to mammals, children, or domestic animals.

How fast can an emergency specialist arrive at my property?

Our active rapid-response dispatch centers run 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. For emergency residential infestations or commercial compliance emergencies, a team will be deployed in specialized vehicles to your site within 60 to 90 minutes.

Is there a written warranty on your pest control treatments?

Yes, all recurring shield contracts and custom bed bug/rodent jobs include our iron-clad Peptop Satisfaction Shield. If targeted pests reappear at any point between your regular treatment cycles, we will deploy our rapid response squad to re-treat your home at absolutely zero additional charge.

What preparations should I make before the inspection squad arrives?

For standard audits, simply clear furniture away from wall perimeters and ensure pets are securely placed in bedrooms. For specialized operations like bed bug thermal convection or deep fogging, your customer coordinator will email you a simple checklist containing detailed step-by-step room prep steps.

What states and counties are actively covered by your squads?

We operate service depots throughout Metro City, Suburbia, Northwood County, Southbay Bay, and Coastal Haven. If you are uncertain about service availability, please insert your ZIP code into our contact form to view direct dispatch depot assignments.
